Prototype of Alpheios Bookmarklet

Desktop Instructions (Safari and Chrome)

  1. Drag the following link to your browser's bookmarks toolbar
    Enable Alpheios!
  2. Navigate to any Greek page - e.g. try Bibliotheca Augustana
  3. Click the Enable Alpheios! Bookmark - you should see a small popup quickly appear and disappear at the top left while the resources are loading
  4. Double click a greek word.

iPhone Instructions (Safari)

  1. Bookmark this page
  2. Highlight and copy the following javascript:
    javascript:void((function() {var element=document.createElement('script'); element.setAttribute('src', 'http://alpheios.net/bookmarklet/scripts/alph.js'); document.body.appendChild(element)})());
  3. Edit the bookmark for this page, replacing the url with the javscript you just copied
  4. Navigate to any Greek page - e.g. try Bibliotheca Augustana
  5. Open your bookmarks and select the one you just edited
  6. You should see a small popup quickly appear and disappear at the top left while the resources are loading
  7. Tap a word on the page
